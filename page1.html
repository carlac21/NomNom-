Tattu £££

Tattu is a modern Chinese cuisine and cocktails restaurant and bar. It has a dark sophisticated interior with an instragam-able floral tree which makes for a beautiful ambience and amazing photos!

Tattu is perfect for drinks for special events, with friends or partners. Enjoy beautiful tasting cocktails from a large assortment ranging from the smoking ‘Skull Candy’ to the ‘Crouching Tiger’.

Crazy Pedros £

If you’re looking for a cheap and cheerful student bar, then Crazy Pedros is the bar for you!

The party never stops at Crazy Pedros and is the perfect place to go with your mates for some yum pizza and drinks!

Check it out, and enjoy!

The Laundrette ££

Cocktails and Carbs! Thats the motto of The Laundrette!

You will not find anywhere else with cooler cocktails! They have cocktails in bathtubs, popcorn tubs and with candyfloss and sweets! Definitely one of the hot spots for cocktail lovers.

This is a hip bar with a heated terrace. You can even enjoy some creative pizza with your creative cocktails and is perfect to go anytime with anyone!
